The Senior
Leadership Intensive

An eight-week modular programme for experienced leaders navigating the step-change into enterprise-level responsibility. Designed for clarity under pressure, strategy under ambiguity, and leadership at scale.

Next cohort May 12, 2026
Duration 8 weeks · modular
Format Residential (On-campus)
Capacity 24 seats / cohort
Fees ₹4,50,000 + GST
Reserve your seat →
01 — Overview

What the programme does.

The Senior Leadership Intensive is designed for leaders who have proven themselves in functional or business-unit roles and are now stepping into enterprise-level responsibility — where the old playbook no longer fits.

Over eight weeks of modular residential sessions, you'll build the thinking, the frameworks, and the peer community to lead at the level your role demands. Faculty are senior practitioners and academic scholars. Every module is anchored in real-world application — not theory for its own sake.

02 — Who it's for

Designed for leaders who —

  • Hold a senior functional or business-unit role and are being prepared for enterprise-level responsibility.
  • Have 12+ years of management experience with clear impact at the P&L or function level.
  • Are moving into roles that require cross-functional judgement, board-facing communication, and multi-horizon strategy.
  • Want a cohort experience — peer learning as much as faculty teaching.
  • Are committed to applying learning between modules, not just attending them.
03 — Curriculum

Eight modules. One arc.

  • 01

    The Leader as Decision-Maker

    Frameworks for decision quality, judgment under uncertainty, and the cognitive biases that derail senior leadership.

  • 02

    Strategic Thinking & Diagnosis

    Reading the competitive landscape, identifying leverage points, and separating signal from noise.

  • 03

    Leading Organizations at Scale

    Organization design, operating models, and the architecture of enterprise-level execution.

  • 04

    Finance for Enterprise Leaders

    Capital allocation, value creation, and the financial fluency the CFO will expect of you.

  • 05

    Leading Through Change

    Transformation, crisis, and the psychology of taking people through uncertainty.

  • 06

    Stakeholder & Board Communication

    Writing, speaking, and presenting at the level your role demands — boards, investors, regulators, media.

  • 07

    Innovation & Growth

    Building the muscle to see new businesses, spin up ventures, and protect the core while growing beyond it.

  • 08

    Capstone & Executive Practicum

    Apply the arc to a real strategic question you bring from your organization. Faculty review, peer challenge, and a presentation to the cohort.

07 — FAQ

Common questions.

Is attendance in-person mandatory for all modules? +
Yes. The residential format is core to the programme's design. We do not offer virtual attendance for the Senior Leadership Intensive.
What happens if I miss a module? +
You may make up a missed module with the next cohort on a case-by-case basis. More than one missed module may require re-enrolment.
Is this programme certified or credentialed? +
You receive a Certificate of Completion from AmpliSkill. The programme is non-accredited and is not currently a formal certification.
Can my organization sponsor me? +
Yes — corporate sponsorship is common. We can invoice your employer directly and provide all necessary documentation.
What's the selection process? +
Application review, a brief conversation with a programme director, and reference checks. We select for cohort balance across industries and functional backgrounds.
