Consulting

Advisory that thinks
and ships.

Five focused practices delivered by senior practitioners. We combine the rigour of academic inquiry with the pragmatism of people who have run large organizations — so you get answers, not just analysis.

Five focused practices

What we consult on.

Every engagement is scoped, staffed, and led by senior team members. No junior-loaded pyramids.

01

CSR Impact
Assessment

Independent assessment of your Corporate Social Responsibility programs — impact measurement, beneficiary outcomes, and reporting aligned to statutory and voluntary frameworks.

  • Theory-of-change mapping
  • Beneficiary surveys & field visits
  • Impact measurement (SROI, IRIS+)
  • Board-ready reports
02

Internal Policy
Review

Independent review of your internal policies — HR, risk, governance, procurement — to assess currency, coherence, and alignment with leading practice and regulation.

  • Policy inventory & gap analysis
  • Benchmarking against peers
  • Redrafting & harmonization
  • Implementation roadmap
03

Employee
Assessment

Structured assessment of employee capability, leadership bench strength, and organizational health — using validated psychometrics, 360° reviews, and simulation-based assessment centres.

  • Individual leadership profiling
  • High-potential identification
  • Assessment & development centres
  • Culture & engagement diagnostics
04

Feasibility
Study

Rigorous pre-investment and pre-commitment studies for new business lines, market entries, geographies, products, or strategic initiatives.

  • Market & demand sizing
  • Competitive & regulatory analysis
  • Financial modelling & scenarios
  • Risk assessment & recommendation
05

Risk & Controls
Assessment

Enterprise and operational risk assessment — identifying key risks, testing the control environment, and recommending improvements aligned to regulatory and board expectations.

  • Enterprise risk assessment
  • Process-level controls testing
  • Regulatory gap & readiness review
  • Board-ready risk reporting
+

Custom
Engagements

Outside our five practice lines but still in our wheelhouse? We take on select custom engagements where senior AmpliSkill team members can lead from the front.

  • Governance reviews
  • Organization design
  • Strategy offsites
  • Public-sector advisory
Our approach

Senior-led, outcome-focused,
evidence-based.

01

Scope

A clear problem statement and success criteria — agreed upfront, in writing. No bait-and-switch.

02

Diagnose

Field work, interviews, data analysis, benchmarks. We look for what's happening — not what we want to find.

03

Design

Recommendations that account for your constraints, your people, and your pace. Strategy that can be implemented.

04

Deliver

We stay for implementation where it makes sense. We leave when your team owns the work — never before.
