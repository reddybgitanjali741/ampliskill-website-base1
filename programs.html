Programs built around your organization.

Every organization carries its own weight — its own culture, constraints, and ambitions. Our custom programs are designed in close partnership with your team, delivered in the format that fits your leaders.

Two delivery formats

Residential or non-residential.

01

On-Campus · Residential

Immersive, multi-day programs delivered at our partner campuses. Full-day cohort learning with evening reflection, peer learning, and faculty office hours.

  • 3–8 day intensives, 1–8 week modular
  • Accommodation, meals, and facilities included
  • Ideal for strategic offsites and cohort bonding
  • Capacity: 15–35 participants
02

Off-Campus · Non-residential

Programs delivered at your offices, partner venues, or virtually — fitting around the demands of your leaders' day jobs.

  • Half-day or full-day sessions, spread over weeks
  • Hybrid and virtual options available
  • Ideal for ongoing capability building
  • Capacity: 10–50+ participants

Our custom engagement model covers:

Industry Training / OJT Research partnerships Consulting engagements Workshops & Seminars Internships University / Resume sharing Extended Diploma (future) Certifications (future)
